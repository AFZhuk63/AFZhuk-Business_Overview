# Founder | Solution Architect | Systems Engineer

## Hippocrates Global

**2025–Present**

**Position**

In 2025, Anatoliy Zhuk founded **Hippocrates Global**, an international software engineering initiative focused on the development of a next-generation AI-ready healthcare platform.

The project represents the culmination of more than forty years of professional experience in engineering, construction, business management, investment planning, international cooperation, and software engineering.

Unlike conventional medical information systems, Hippocrates Global is being designed as a modular digital healthcare ecosystem capable of supporting multiple healthcare providers, jurisdictions, and specialized medical products within a unified cloud-native architecture.

The project follows a long-term engineering strategy emphasizing software architecture before feature implementation, with particular attention to scalability, maintainability, information security, and international deployment.

- Domain-Driven Design (DDD);
- Secure-by-Design Architecture;

- Infrastructure as Code;
